#Nanodegree Arcade Game
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------



##Installation:
----------------

* Download the repository by clicking on green highlited button:
* Click download ZIP on the right of the screen, then extract the zip file to your computer.
* Navigate to where you unzipped the file.
* Double-click index.html to open the game in your browser.
* Enjoy the game.

##Gameplay Instuction:
------------------------

1. Use the arrow keys to move.
2. The main objective is to reach the top of the water to score.
3. Each time you reach the top of the water your score increases. 
4. Just be safe from enemies they can kill you, you have to start from initial stage.
